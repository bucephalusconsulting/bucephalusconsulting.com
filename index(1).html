What We Do

Services

Practical, expert guidance for the regulatory and planning challenges that matter most to your community.

Heritage Planning

Heritage Impact Assessments, Cultural Heritage Landscape evaluations, and advisory services for Part IV and Part V designations under the Ontario Heritage Act. We help municipalities and proponents navigate heritage planning with clarity.

Archaeological Assessment

Stage 1–2 archaeological assessment oversight, peer review, and advisory services. We ensure compliance with provincial standards and provide expert analysis to support planning decisions and Ontario Land Tribunal proceedings.

Environmental Screening

Environmental screening for Endangered Species Act and Planning Act applications, natural heritage system evaluation, and policy review. We help planning departments and developers identify constraints early and move projects forward.

Policy & Peer Review

Official Plan conformity reviews for cultural and natural heritage policies, expert peer review of technical reports, and support for legal professionals requiring independent analysis for tribunals and hearings.
