What We Do

Services

Practical, expert guidance for the regulatory and planning challenges that matter most to your community.

Heritage Planning

Heritage Impact Assessments, Cultural Heritage Landscape evaluations, and advisory services for Part IV and Part V designations under the Ontario Heritage Act. We help municipalities and proponents navigate heritage planning with clarity.

Archaeological Assessment

Stage 1–2 archaeological assessment oversight, peer review, and advisory services. We ensure compliance with provincial standards and provide expert analysis to support planning decisions and Ontario Land Tribunal proceedings.

Environmental Screening

Environmental screening for Endangered Species Act and Planning Act applications, natural heritage system evaluation, and policy review. We help planning departments and developers identify constraints early and move projects forward.

Land Use Planning

Official Plan and Zoning By-law review, development application support, and planning policy analysis. We help municipalities and applicants navigate the planning approvals process with practical, informed guidance.

Natural Heritage & Ecology

Natural heritage system screening, ecological constraint analysis, and environmental impact assessment support. We identify natural heritage features early in the planning process to reduce risk and avoid delays.

Policy & Peer Review

Official Plan conformity reviews for cultural and natural heritage policies, expert peer review of technical reports, and independent analysis for legal professionals preparing for tribunals and hearings.

About the Firm

Full-spectrum heritage and environmental expertise.

Bucephalus Consulting was founded to give municipalities, legal professionals, and development teams access to the kind of specialized heritage and environmental planning expertise that is typically locked up in large firms — without the overhead, the bureaucracy, or the wait.

Our team brings together advanced academic training in environmental studies, ecology, and archaeology with years of hands-on experience managing heritage and environmental programs on major public-sector infrastructure accounts. We understand how planning decisions are made at every level — municipal, provincial, and tribunal — and we bring that perspective to every engagement.

Our expertise spans:

  • Heritage conservation planning and Ontario Heritage Act advisory
  • Archaeological assessment oversight and peer review
  • Environmental screening, ecological assessment, and species-at-risk
  • Land use planning, official plan policy, and development review
  • Ontario Land Tribunal expert support and testimony
  • Public infrastructure and asset management advisory
  • Academic research and professional development
